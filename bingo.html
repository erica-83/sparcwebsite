SPARC Weekly Bingo

Join us every week for a fun-filled game of Bingo with the SPARC community! Sign up below to claim your card and get the Zoom link.

Sign Up for Bingo

How It Works

It's easy to join SPARC Bingo!

Step 1: Sign Up

Fill out the form below with your name, email, and choose an available bingo card number (1-30).

Step 2: Get Your Card

We'll email you a confirmation with your bingo card and the Zoom meeting link to join the game.

Step 3: Play!

Join the Zoom call at game time with your bingo card ready. First to get BINGO wins!

Sign Up for This Week's Bingo

Cards reset every Friday at midnight. Choose an available card number below!

Bingo Rules

1
Each player can only sign up for one card per week.
2
Cards reset every Friday at midnight - sign up early!
3
Join the Zoom call with your camera on so we can see you.
4
Mark your card as numbers are called out.
5
Shout "BINGO!" when you complete a line or pattern.
6
Have fun and be respectful to other players!

Questions About Bingo?

Contact us if you have any questions or need help signing up.
