Strategic Plan

Our roadmap for growth, impact, and community transformation

Our Vision for the Future

"A Northern Virginia community where every adult with intellectual and developmental disabilities has access to meaningful programs, genuine friendships, and opportunities to thrive."

SPARC's strategic plan guides our efforts to expand our reach, deepen our impact, and ensure long-term sustainability. Developed with input from families, staff, board members, and community stakeholders, this plan reflects our commitment to growth while maintaining the quality programming that defines SPARC.

Strategic Pillars

Our strategic plan is built on four core pillars that guide our decision-making and resource allocation.

1

Program Excellence

Deliver exceptional, person-centered programming that meets the diverse needs of adults with disabilities and their families.

  • Enhance life skills curriculum
  • Increase community integration opportunities
  • Develop specialized programs for different ability levels
2

Geographic Growth

Expand our footprint to serve more communities across Northern Virginia and reduce waitlists.

  • Open Arlington location in 2025
  • Explore Prince William County expansion
  • Increase capacity at existing centers
  • Develop satellite programming partnerships
3

Financial Sustainability

Build a diversified funding base to ensure long-term stability and support continued growth.

  • Grow individual donor base by 50%
  • Increase corporate partnerships
  • Build operating reserve fund
4

Organizational Capacity

Invest in staff, systems, and infrastructure to support quality programming and sustainable growth.

  • Enhance staff compensation and benefits
  • Implement professional development programs
  • Upgrade technology and data systems
  • Strengthen governance and leadership pipeline

Key Initiatives 2024-2027

2024

Foundation Year

  • Herndon Center Launch: Successfully opened our fourth location
  • Technology Upgrade: Implemented new participant management system
  • Record Fundraising: Raised $150,000 at An Evening to SPARCle
Completed
2025

Expansion Year

  • Alexandria and Loudoun County: Launch centers in Alexandria and Loudoun County
  • Staff Development: Implement leadership training program
  • Partnership Growth: Establish 5 new corporate partnerships
  • Higher Education Partnership: Partner with a university to enhance programs
Completed
2026

Deepening Year

  • Program Specialization: Launch specialized programming tracks
  • Increase Referrals: Strengthen referral pathways and reduce systemic barriers to access
  • Website Relaunch: Launch new accessible, user-friendly website
  • Establish Corporate Investments: Secure partners to fully fund each center
In Progress
2027

Scaling Year

  • Regional Expansion: Evaluate Prince William County
  • Model Replication: Document SPARC model for potential replication
  • Endowment Launch: Establish organizational endowment
Planned
