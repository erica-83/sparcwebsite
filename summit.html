About the Summit

Adults with significant and multiple disabilities experience some of the highest levels of social isolation, limited access to meaningful daytime engagement, and families living with constant financial, emotional, and caregiving strain.

The SPARC Summit brings together families, advocates, researchers, policymakers, and community partners for a one-day working forum focused on solutions and collective action.

Sponsorship reflects a shared commitment to advancing equity, rights, and belonging for some of the most isolated adults in our communities.
