An Evening to SPARCle

Our signature fundraising event brings together participants, families, staff, donors, and community leaders to celebrate our impact and raise funds for the year ahead.

The event features raffles, entertainment, and heartfelt stories from the SPARC community. It's our largest fundraising event of the year.
