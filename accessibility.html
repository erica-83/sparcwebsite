Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to digital accessibility for all users

Our Commitment

SPARC (Specially Adapted Resource Centers) is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We believe that all individuals, regardless of ability, should have equal access to the information and services provided on our website.

As an organization that serves adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities, we understand firsthand the importance of accessible digital experiences. We continually work to improve the accessibility of our website and apply relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

We regularly review our website to identify and address accessibility barriers. Our commitment to accessibility is ongoing, and we strive to continuously improve the user experience for all visitors.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard Navigation: All pages and interactive elements can be navigated using a keyboard alone
  • Skip-to-Content Links: Skip navigation links allow users to bypass repetitive content
  • Alternative Text: Images include descriptive alternative text for screen reader users
  • Video Accessibility: Videos include captions and transcripts where available
  • Color Contrast: Text and background colors meet WCAG 2.1 AA contrast requirements (4.5:1 minimum for normal text)
  • Resizable Text: Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content or functionality
  • Clear Headings: Proper heading hierarchy helps users understand page structure and navigate content
  • Form Labels: Form fields include clear labels and instructions
  • Focus Indicators: Visible focus outlines help keyboard users track their position on the page
  • Responsive Design: The website adapts to different screen sizes and devices

Assistive Technology Compatibility

This website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (including VoiceOver, NVDA, and JAWS)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation

Location Accessibility

We provide accessibility information for each of our physical locations on our Locations page. This includes details about wheelchair accessibility, accessible parking, and other accommodation features.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some content may not yet be fully accessible:

  • PDF Documents: Some older PDF documents may not be fully accessible. We are working to update these. Please contact us if you need an accessible version of any document.
  • Third-Party Content: Some embedded content from third-party platforms may have accessibility limitations beyond our control.

Continuous Improvement

SPARC is committed to the ongoing improvement of our website's accessibility. We regularly:

  • Conduct accessibility audits using automated tools and manual testing
  • Train our staff on accessibility best practices
  • Review and update our content to meet accessibility standards
  • Incorporate accessibility feedback from users
