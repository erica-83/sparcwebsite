The Access Point

Accessibility Reimagined

Real conversations about real barriers. Join SPARC participants as they share their experiences, challenge assumptions, and redefine what's possible for adults with disabilities. Every episode is a step toward a more inclusive world.

Meet Your Host

Kat Rader, MAT

Host of The Access Point

Kat brings warmth, curiosity, and a passion for authentic storytelling to every episode of The Access Point. With a gift for creating space where guests feel comfortable sharing their truths, Kat guides conversations that go beyond surface-level discussions of disability to explore the real experiences, challenges, and triumphs of SPARC participants. Each episode is an invitation to see the world through a different lens.
