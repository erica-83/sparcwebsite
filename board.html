Board of Directors

Dedicated leaders volunteering their expertise to guide SPARC's mission and ensure sustainable growth

Leadership That Makes a Difference

Our Board of Directors brings together experienced professionals from diverse backgrounds who share a common passion: creating opportunities for adults with severe and multiple disabilities to thrive in their communities. Their guidance, expertise, and dedication help ensure SPARC continues to grow and serve Northern Virginia effectively.
