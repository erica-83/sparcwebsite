Volunteer Opportunities

Program Support

Assist staff with daily activities including arts and crafts, games, music, and community outings. Work directly with participants to help them engage and thrive.

3-4 hours/week, weekdays

Special Skills

Lead a workshop in your area of expertise - art, music, cooking, fitness, technology, or other skills. Share your passion with our community!

Flexible, project-based

Event Support

Help with special events like An Evening to SPARCle, holiday celebrations, and community awareness events. Perfect for one-time volunteering!

Event-based

Administrative

Help with office tasks, mailings, data entry, and organizational projects. Perfect for volunteers who prefer behind-the-scenes support.

Flexible hours
